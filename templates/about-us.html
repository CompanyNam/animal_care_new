Who we are?

Animal Care Club (ACC) was created to help sick or injured stray animals. Animal Care Club works to promote the human-animal bond, protect street animals, support animal welfare, stop wildlife abuse, confront cruelty to animals in all of its forms. Our work includes group visits to animal shelters, ecological projects, and educating people mercy towards animals in our community.

   Visit to the stray dogs shelter located in Razin region. 

The club consists of curators and volunteers. We have come together to protest animal cruelty and violence, to educate youth and to rescue the lives. Our mission is to reach out our helping hand to our four-legged friends.

   Taking action against cruel cull of stray dogs next to “Sobachiy Yashik” - a place where all stray dogs are brought and killed by municipalities in Balakhany

 

We work to improve the welfare of pets by:

  • Ensuring that every pet is cared for properly and has a good home
  • Ending cruelty to pet animals
  • Raising standards for pet animals in Azerbaijan
  • Stopping pet overpopulation

These are just four of our goals to help pet animals.

   Feeding campaign with club volunteers at the shelter

 

Animal Care Club does not operate under any sponsor support. We receive financial aid for the animals from charity campaigns and events that we organize ourselves.

 Charity sale at French-Azerbaijani University. Books, vegan and vegeterian food were introduced for the visitors

 

There are a number of individuals and organizations supporting our work, including Azerbaijani-French University, French Ambassador Aurelia Bouchez, Morocco Ambassador Mohammad Adil Embarsh, Icherisheher municipality, VarYox media group, Cult Baku private members club.

 French Ambassador Aurelia Bouchez and the founder of Doggie Styles couture Naila Aliyeva taking picture with Ned The Dog at our charity concert “Be their voice!” organized in Landmark Hotel on New Year’s eve

 

We are glad to cooperate with the ones who share our main goal: enthusiastic people who are concerned with animal abuse and crue
